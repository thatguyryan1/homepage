Back-End Code

Another coding term that I define as a part of a piece that is never seen but keeps it functioning. Ex. sketches and rough drafts of a piece.

Binary code

A language that computers simply understand as true or false questions in the use of 1 being on and 0 being off. Something that can’t be applied though to the living organisms as people don’t see the world as black and white but more of different shades of gray.

Dead of Night

The time at which I am the most productive in my art. It is a time in which there is no noise or light as everyone is asleep leaving nothing to distract me from my goals.

Depressing

Causing a loss of hope or sadness.

Desaturated

a loss of color saturation resulting in it looking dull.

Friends

A great source of feedback as well as inspiration for my art ideas. Whenever I get stuck and don’t have access to a professor’s help, I go to them as I trust them to help me.

Front-End Code

While originating from coding, my definition is best described as the part of an art piece that is seen by all. Ex. the final product of a painting.

Function

A use, a reason for existence. Something I feel I am constantly trying to find.

Realism

A style characterized by copying the natural world. The style that got me interested in art originally as a kid.
