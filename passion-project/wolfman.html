The Wolfman

The Wolf Man is another Universal film created in 1941 and written by Curt Siodmak. The film is where a lot of many werewolf tropes actually started such as silver being its weaknest. It also shows the wolfman as a more humanoid animal rather than being more wolf hence the term "Wolfman" which I found pretty interesting.
